
Project Name: Tropical Oasis
Location: Vennala, Kochi
Area: 1500 sq.ft
about the project 
Tropical-eclectic interiors blending jewel-toned furniture (forest green, navy, deep teal) with soft neutral bases, botanical mural wallpapers, arched wall niches, and warm rattan/wood accents. The palette leans rich and nature-inspired, balancing bold color statements with relaxed, resort-style comfort.
