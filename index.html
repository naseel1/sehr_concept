About Us

Reimagining Realities: Visualization Redefined

Welcome to Sehr Concepts — where dreams become reality, and spaces come to life. We believe that every space has the potential to inspire, delight, and enhance the lives of those who inhabit it. With a passion for creativity and a commitment to excellence, we specialize in crafting bespoke interiors that reflect our clients' unique personalities and lifestyles.

Our team of designers brings a wealth of experience and expertise to every project, from luxurious residential homes to dynamic commercial spaces. With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to exceptional craftsmanship, we transform ordinary environments into extraordinary showcases of style and sophistication.

Driven by a deep understanding of design principles and a genuine desire to exceed expectations, we collaborate closely with our clients to bring their visions to life. Whether it's revamping a single room or redesigning an entire property, we approach each project with creativity, integrity, and a relentless pursuit of perfection.

Join us on a journey of inspiration, innovation, and imagination as we redefine the art of interior design.

“At Sehr Concepts, we don't just create beautiful spaces — we create lasting impressions.” The Sehr Promise
Our Mission & Vision

What Guides Every Line We Draw

Two ideas steer every project we touch — how we see the future of a space, and how we work to get there.

Vision

To redefine how spaces are imagined — turning every blank canvas into an immersive story of light, material, and emotion, visualized long before a single wall is built.

Mission

To craft bespoke interiors and photorealistic visualizations that give our clients the confidence to build boldly — guided by quality, trust, passion, innovation, and integrity in everything we create.

What We Do

Our Services

From first sketch to final render, three disciplines carry every project forward.

01

Concept Design

Indulge in the elegance of our bespoke interior design services, tailored to elevate your space with refinement and luxury.

02

Schematic Design

Developing floor plans, elevations, and more to illustrate the layout of the space, along with cost estimates & timelines for the project.

03

3D Visualization

Immersive & interactive computer-generated 3D visualization of spaces to accurately depict layout, materials, lighting, and furniture before construction begins.

03

Architectural Planning

Structural drawings, layout approvals, and technical documentation that ensure your design is buildable, compliant, and ready for construction.

03

Turnkey Projects

End-to-end execution from groundwork to final handover —construction, material sourcing, and installation managed under one roof, delivered fully finished and move-in ready.

The People Behind SEHR

Meet Our Founders

Two visionaries, one philosophy—creating timeless spaces through thoughtful design, precision, and craftsmanship.

Co-Founder & Design Director

Amal Radhakrishnan

Amal brings over seven years of experience in architecture and interior design, having worked with leading real estate developers in India, a luxury furniture brand, and a top UAE property developer.

His expertise spans architectural planning, interior design, material selection, project coordination, and turnkey execution. Guided by a philosophy of functionality, timeless aesthetics, and meticulous attention to detail, Amal leads SEHR Concepts in creating elegant, practical, and enduring spaces tailored to every client's vision.

Co-Founder & Operations Director

Thoyyib NM

Thoyyib brings over seven years of experience in the interior design industry, having worked with several established design studios, including a leading premium villa developer in Kochi.

He oversees SEHR Concepts' day-to-day operations, managing project planning, budgets, timelines, procurement, and vendor coordination. His commitment to precision, quality, and efficiency ensures every residential, retail, and hospitality project is delivered seamlessly.
