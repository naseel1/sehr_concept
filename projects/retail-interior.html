Project Overview

Editorial Contemporary Retail Interior

Designed as a refined retail destination, this project balances contemporary elegance with warm earthy materials to create an immersive customer experience.

Warm terracotta, rust, and burnt-orange tones anchor the interior against soft beige walls and terrazzo flooring. Deep red beam ceilings with mesh infill panels create rhythm overhead, while curved metal garment rails, brass-framed display cases, and sculptural cube plinths transform the space into an editorial boutique rather than a conventional retail store.

Every design decision focuses on enhancing the shopping experience through spatial flow, premium materials, layered lighting and thoughtfully curated display systems.
