Project Overview

Tropical Eclectic Residence

Tropical Oasis is a vibrant residential interior that celebrates nature-inspired living through rich colours, handcrafted textures, and a relaxed resort-style atmosphere.

The interiors combine soft neutral backgrounds with statement furniture in forest green, navy, and deep teal, creating spaces that feel both elegant and welcoming. Botanical mural wallpapers, sculpted arched niches, and carefully layered décor bring a sense of character while maintaining visual harmony throughout the home.

Warm rattan detailing, natural wood finishes, and abundant greenery complete the tropical aesthetic, delivering a home that feels calm, luxurious, and deeply connected to nature. Every space is designed to offer everyday comfort while reflecting the relaxed sophistication of a boutique resort.
